What's New in CotEditor 2.1

Autosave
CotEditor now automatically saves your documents while editing.
cot command-line tool
The new cot command-line tool lets you open documents in CotEditor from the command line. You can install it from Integration pane in Preferences.
Brand-new Find & Replace panel
The Find and Replace panel has been updated with a new, more modern look.
New document inspector view
The legacy document information drawer was now migrated to a side panel style. This change allows you to see a documents information in full-screen mode.
